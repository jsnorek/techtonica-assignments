Chocolate Chip Cookies GF/V

These are a delicious, healthier option when you have a sweet tooth craving!

Photo Credit: Sally's Baking Recipes

Recipe

  • 1/3 cup solidified coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 tablespoons almond milk
  • 1 cup oat flour
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Combine coconut oil and sugar and mix on high for 1 minute.
  2. Add vanilla and almond milk and mix for 30 seconds.
  3. Add almond flour with the mixer running.
  4. Add oat flour and dry ingredients.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 12-13 minutes.
