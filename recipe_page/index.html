Chocolate Chip Cookies GF/V

These are a delicious, healthier option when you have a sweet tooth craving!

Photo Credit: Wikipedia

Recipe

  • 1/3 cup solidified coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 tablespoons almond milk
  • 1 cup oat flour
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup chopped raw pecans

Instructions

  • Combine coconut oil and sugar and mix on high for 1 minute.
  • Add vanilla and almond milk and mix for 30 seconds.
  • Add almond flour with the mixer running.
  • Add oat flour and dry ingredients.
  • Bake at 350 degrees for 12-13 minutes.
